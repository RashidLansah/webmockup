WHY

Sports and music are the sparks, empowerment
and inclusion set fire.

In too many countries, deaf and blind young people are forgotten altogether. Even though
these young people are amazingly cool and have unique talents to match. That is why Memphis
Foundation is investing in a world where deaf and blind young people can fully participate and
are completely welcome in their community. Where they can show their talents and make
dreams come true. To achieve that, we use the power of music and sports.
